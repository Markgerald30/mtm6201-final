Our Story

Green Leaf Vegetarian was founded in 2015 by Ottawa native Maya Sinclair, who dreamed of bringing farm-fresh, plant-based flavors to her hometown. We partner with local farmers for seasonal produce, craft every dish in-house, and take sustainability as seriously as taste. Whether you're vegan, vegetarian, or just veggie-curious, our menu invites you on a journey of bold, wholesome flavors.

Since our opening, we've grown from a single storefront into a vibrant community hub—hosting weekly "Meet the Farmer" dinners, seasonal cooking workshops, and charity pop-ups to support local food banks. Our chefs continually experiment with new ingredients and global techniques, ensuring the menu evolves with the seasons and your tastes. We've even launched a zero-waste program that turns kitchen scraps into compost for our partnered farms and donates surplus meals to neighbours in need. At Green Leaf Vegetarian, every meal is an invitation—to savor great food, to connect with like-minded food lovers, and to make a positive impact on both plate and planet.
