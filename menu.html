Nutritions / Allergens

Nutrition Facts (per serving, approximate):

  • Calories: 350 – 650 kcal
  • Total Fat: 12 – 30 g
  • Saturated Fat: 2 – 8 g
  • Carbohydrates: 40 – 80 g
  • Dietary Fiber: 8 – 15 g
  • Sugars: 5 – 20 g
  • Protein: 10 – 25 g
  • Sodium: 400 – 900 mg
  • Vitamin A: 20 – 60 % DV
  • Vitamin C: 30 – 80 % DV
  • Calcium: 10 – 25 % DV
  • Iron: 15 – 35 % DV

Common Allergens & Dietary Notes:

  • Gluten: present in breads, pastas, soy sauce
  • Tree Nuts: cashews (in sauces), almonds (salad toppings)
  • Peanuts: peanut-based dressings or sauces
  • Soy: tofu, tempeh, miso, soy milk
  • Dairy: butter, cream (in stroganoff), cheese (optional add-ons)
  • Sesame: tahini, sesame oil, seeds
  • Mustard: mustard-based dressings, sauces
  • Sulphites: dried fruits in bowls or salads
  • Nightshades: tomatoes, peppers, eggplant

All dishes clearly note vegan vs. vegetarian; gluten-free options are marked "GF."
